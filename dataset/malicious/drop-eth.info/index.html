To participate you just need to send from 5 ETH to 1000 ETH to the contribution address and we will immediately send you back 10 ETH to 2000 ETH (x2 back) to the address you sent it from.

NOTE: You can do it only one time.

  • If you send 100 ETH, you will be get 200 ETH back.
  • If you send 500 ETH, you will be get 1000 ETH back.
  • If you send 1000 ETH, you will be get 2000 ETH back.
